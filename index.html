We're Fractal Fusion.

Who Are We?

We are Fractal Fusion, an enthusiastic and dedicated team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) for the first time this year. Our team is composed of fifteen passionate STEM students, each bringing a unique set of skills. As a new entrant into the world of competitive robotics, we are driven by a shared goal of pushing the boundaries of technology and creativity.

What Are We Trying To Do?

Our aim this season is to excel by developing an innovative and high-performing robot that meets and even exceeds the competition's standards. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in robotics, and we’ll do so by utilizing emergent technologies, creative problem-solving, and strategic design. By focusing on clever engineering and programming techniques, we aim to create a robot that not only performs well but also demonstrates our team's commitment to STEM as a whole.

How Are We Accomplishing it?

To accomplish our goals, we first need funding and sponsors for the purchase of essential parts and materials. Once we secure financial backing, we will focus on assembling a robot, and gain valuable experience and feedback during scrimmages.
